# Community groups

Oxford Road is part of a wider community of residents' associations and groups
in the area. There are regular events, meetings and socials throughout the year
that are open to all.

## St Augustine's Church

Our AGMs and many other local events such as community groups, evening talks,
concerts and more are held at [St Augustine's Church](https://stas.org.uk/).
Check their website for upcoming events, socials and meetings.

[Visit stas.org.uk &rarr;](https://stas.org.uk/)

## Friends of Histon Road Rec

FoHRR is a group interested in the use, enhancement and protection of the
recreation ground. They advocate for improvements to the rec, carry out projects
in partnership with the City Council, and organise events on the rec such as
their annual easter egg hunt. Find out more on their website or keep an eye on
our mailing list where we publicise events.

[Visit fohrr.org.uk &rarr;](https://fohrr.org.uk/)

## Darwin Green Residents' Association

DGRA is a neighbouring residents' association at Darwin Green. They have a wealth
of knowledge on their website, including lots of information about the leisure and
community facilities at Darwin Green.

[Visit dgra.cam &rarr;](https://dgra.cam/)
