# History of Oxford Road

Oxford Road has a rich local history shaped by the growth of Cambridge over the decades.

## Capturing Cambridge: Oxford Road

The Museum of Cambridge maintains a collection of local stories, photographs, and memories from residents as part of their [Capturing Cambridge](https://capturingcambridge.org/) project. The project documents the history of Cambridge through the people who live here and includes historical photographs, personal recollections, and accounts of how the area has changed over time.

For Oxford Road, some entries include previous residents and photographs of them. If you click on the "Oxford Road" entry you will find some history of the estate and some wonderful memories from Agnes Newling.

If you have memories or photographs to add, you can submit them. It's also worth exploring other roads in the Castle area.

[Visit the Capturing Cambridge: Oxford Road project &rarr;](https://capturingcambridge.org/projects/oxford-road/)

## Historical Development

There is also an interesting summary of the historical development of the Huntingdon Road area included in this report from Cambridge City Council. Although mostly intended as an aid for conservation decisions it contains much detail about how the area evolved over time.


[Cambridge Suburbs and Approaches - Huntingdon Road (pdf) &rarr;](
