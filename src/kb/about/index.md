# About ORRA

The Oxford Road Residents Association is a community group dedicated to improving
and maintaining the quality of life on Oxford Road and the surrounding area.

## The committee

ORRA is run by a volunteer committee elected at our Annual General Meeting. We
have up to 10 committee members including the following named positions:

- **Co-Chair:** Emily Calderbank-May
- **Co-Chair:** Olly Morris
- **Secretary:** Natalia Golowicz
- **Treasurer:** Oli Lane

However, we also regularly co-opt other residents to help out on specific issues,
projects and causes. Do get in touch if you'd like to get involved in any
capacity.
