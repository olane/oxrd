# Community petition: stop the rat running

We need your support to tackle the growing problem of traffic on Oxford Road.
Increasingly, our street is being used as a rat run to the A14, bringing more
vehicles, more risk, and more disruption to our daily lives.

## Why this matters

Traffic pressures are only set to rise with:

- Phase Two of the Eddington development and Darwin Green
- Proposed development of fields off Storey's Way

Without action, Oxford Road risks becoming a major through-route rather than the
residential street we value.

## What we're doing

We have submitted a petition to the council to formally recognise the problem.
We need a minimum of 50 signatures — this is the first step toward getting the
issue acknowledged and addressed.

## Possible solutions

No final decision has been made, but one option being considered is Automatic
Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) at key points, which would:

- Allow access for residents
- Discourage non-local through traffic
- Improve safety for pedestrians, cyclists, and school children

## Why your support matters

If you are concerned about any of the following, please add your name:

- Safety for you and your family
- Children travelling to school
- Noise and air pollution
- Increasing traffic levels
- The long-term impact on our community

## Sign the petition

It only takes a minute. Please share with neighbours, friends, and family to
help build momentum.
