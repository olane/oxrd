Community & events

Oxford Road is part of a wider community of residents' associations and groups in the area. There are regular events, meetings and socials throughout the year that are open to all.

St Augustine's Church

Our AGMs and many other local events such as community groups, evening talks, concerts and more are held at St Augustine's Church. Check their website for upcoming events, socials and meetings.

Visit stas.org.uk →

Friends of Histon Road Rec

FoHRR is a group interested in the use, enhancement and protection of the recreation ground. They advocate for improvements to the rec, carry out projects in partnership with the City Council, and organise events on the rec such as their annual easter egg hunt. Find out more on their website or keep an eye on our mailing list where we publicise events.

Visit fohrr.org.uk →
