Welcome

Welcome to the Oxford Road Residents Association (ORRA). We are a community group dedicated to improving and maintaining the quality of life on Oxford Road and the surrounding area.

What We Do

  • Organise community events and meetings
  • Liaise with the local council on planning and infrastructure
  • Share local news and updates
  • Support neighbours and build community spirit

Current Campaign: Traffic Petition

We're campaigning to tackle the growing problem of rat-running traffic along Oxford Road. We've submitted a petition to the council and need your support.

Read more and sign the petition →

Get in Touch

We also have a street WhatsApp group which you are very welcome to join. Again, just get in touch via email and we will invite you.
