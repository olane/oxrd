Welcome

Welcome to the Oxford Road Residents Association (ORRA). We are a community group dedicated to improving and maintaining the quality of life on Oxford Road and the surrounding area.

What We Do

  • Organise community events and meetings
  • Liaise with the local council on planning and infrastructure
  • Share local news and updates
  • Support neighbours and build community spirit
